Bel's Blurbs

About me:

My name is Isabel but I go by Bel. I am the fourth of five children and a fun fact is that I share my birthday with my older sister!

I am from a small town in New Jersey and moved to Tampa, FL for school. I have been a competitive Irish Dancer for 16 years but recently retired to focus on building my career.

I enjoy traveling and exploring new places. I have traveled to the Caribbean, South America, and Europe. I hope that by the time I am 50 I will have traveled most of the world. I love spending time at the beach reading or surfing, and I enjoy learning new activities or sports.

I am currently learning Japanese and am able to speak a little Spanish. I hope to one day be fluent in both languages and possibly others as well.

Who I'd like to meet:

I'd like to meet people who educate and inspire me as well as enjoy exploring new places and trying new things.
